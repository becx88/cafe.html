Lemon Ricotta Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  4. 3 large eggs
  5. 1½ cups ricotta cheese
  6. Zest of 2 lemons
  7. ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  8. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. 2 tsp baking powder
  10. ¼ tsp salt
  11. Powdered sugar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add ricotta, beat until smooth. Add eggs one at a time.
  4. Mix in lemon zest, juice, and vanilla.
  5. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt separately. Add to wet mix.
  6. Pour batter into pan. Smooth top.
  7. Bake 45–50 mins until golden and toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Cool in pan 15 mins, then on wire rack. Dust with powdered sugar.
